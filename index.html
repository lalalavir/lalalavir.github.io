Nice to meet you 👋

I am a third-year undergraduate student in Actuarial Science at Zhongnan University of Economics and Law (2023–2027), and currently a research intern at the Qingtian Research Institute of Cardinal Operations (Shanshu Technology).

My research builds GPU-accelerated algorithms for large-scale combinatorial optimization—designing parallel solvers that turn classical operations-research methods (Lagrangian relaxation, metaheuristics, local search) into massively parallel GPU pipelines, and bringing them into practical solvers. I am also interested in LLM-based agents for operations management.

I am always happy to chat about research or collaboration—feel free to reach out.
